Creating a strategy to enter the digital transformation

Course: KTH ME2095

When online encyclopedias started to emerge, the company Brockhaus lost a huge amount of market shares. In order to deal with this they needed to go through a digital transformation. A case study has been made on how the company could ideally handle the situation and what strategies that would be most beneficial.

From our analysis of the situation, our recommendation for Brockhaus was to lean towards a migration strategy and focus all investments towards the online market and therefore keep the physical platform as it was and risk it to be overtaken by other competitors. This decision was based on user behavior and a forecast of what the future will hold. Becoming a tech company is of the utmost importance for the company. From the analysis, our recommendation was also to primarily focus on the education and learning segment and in that way find their niche within the online encyclopedia market as a Business-2-Government company. In order to be able to make these changes, recruitment of new talent or proper training of the current employees is a high priority.
